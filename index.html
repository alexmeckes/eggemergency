How to Play

Use arrow keys to move left and right, SPACE to jump (double jump available!)

Catch eggs and press E to transfer them to your basket. Chain transfers for combos!

Golden eggs are worth 3 points! Avoid bomb eggs! Collect power-ups for special abilities.
